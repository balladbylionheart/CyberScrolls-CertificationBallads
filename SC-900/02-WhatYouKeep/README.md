# 🎵 Scroll II — What You Keep  
**SC-900 | Customer Responsibilities in the Cloud**

---

## 📖 Concept Overview

No matter which cloud service model you choose (**IaaS, PaaS, or SaaS**), certain responsibilities always remain with the **customer**.

While the cloud provider manages the underlying infrastructure, you are always responsible for how your environment is used, secured, and configured.

---

## 🧠 Responsibilities You Always Keep

### 📊 Data
You are responsible for your data at all times:

- Classification  
- Protection  
- Encryption choices  
- Governance  

👉 What you store and share is always your responsibility.

---

### 👤 Identity and Access
You control who can access your resources:

- User accounts  
- Permissions and roles  
- Authentication (proving identity)  
- Authorization (defining access)

👉 You decide who gets access and how far that access goes.

---

### 💻 Endpoints
Devices accessing cloud services must be secured:

- Laptops, desktops, and mobile devices  
- Managed and unmanaged endpoints  
- Security posture of devices  

👉 Every device connecting to your environment must be protected.

---

### ⚙️ Configuration
You define how your environment is set up:

- Security settings  
- Tenant configuration  
- Policies and controls  

👉 Misconfiguration is still your responsibility, even in the cloud.

---

## 📈 Key Takeaway

No matter the service model:

- **IaaS**  
- **PaaS**  
- **SaaS**  

These responsibilities always remain with you:

- Data  
- Identity and access  
- Endpoints  
- Configuration  

The provider secures the underlying infrastructure,  
but you are responsible for how your environment is used.
